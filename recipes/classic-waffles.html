Classic Waffles

Description

This Classic Waffle Recipe makes perfectly crisp on the outside, fluffy on the inside waffles that are to die for!!


The Secret To Successful Waffles

Are you ready for the secret trick that makes these waffles the absolute BEST?!

Warm Milk.

Yup, that’s it! And here’s why – the warm milk allows the melted butter to stay in a liquid, without seizing up and clumping. This means the butter is woven beautifully through each and every waffle, every little waffle pocket and you can really taste it! To warm the milk, I simply measure it out into a microwaveable measuring cup and heat it in the microwave for just 1 minute. You don’t want it hot, just warm.


Ingredients

  • 2 Cups all purpose flour – some have used a combination of whole wheat flour and all purpose flour with great success
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 4 tsp baking powder – this is what helps give the waffles their perfect rise!
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 6 tbsp butter - salted or unsalted
  • 1 1/2 cups milk - either 2% or whole milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. In a large bowl combine the flour, sugar, baking powder and salt and whisk to combine.
  3. In a separate small bowl whisk the eggs and stir in the warm milk, melted butter and vanilla extract. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry and whisk until blended.
  4. Scoop the batter into the preheated waffle iron and cook until the waffles are golden brown3 and crisp. Serve immediately or lay on a cooling rack until cool. Store in zip close bags in freezer.
